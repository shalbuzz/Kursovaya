Our Story

Founded in 2005, CableVision started with a simple mission: to provide high-quality television services at affordable prices. What began as a small local cable provider has grown into one of the nation's leading cable and television service companies.

Over the years, we've expanded our service offerings to include high-speed internet, digital phone services, and streaming options, always staying at the forefront of technological advancements in the industry.

Today, we serve over 2 million households across the country, bringing entertainment, information, and connectivity to families everywhere.

Our Technology

State-of-the-art infrastructure powering your entertainment

Fiber-Optic Network

Our advanced fiber-optic network spans over 50,000 miles across the country, delivering lightning-fast speeds and crystal-clear picture quality to millions of homes.

We continuously invest in upgrading our infrastructure to ensure we can handle the growing bandwidth demands of modern entertainment.

Data Centers

Our state-of-the-art data centers are strategically located throughout the country to ensure minimal latency and maximum uptime for all our services.

These facilities are equipped with redundant power systems, advanced cooling technologies, and 24/7 security to protect our network infrastructure.

Set-Top Boxes

Our latest generation of set-top boxes combines powerful hardware with intuitive software to deliver a seamless viewing experience.

Features include 4K Ultra HD support, voice control, integrated streaming apps, and advanced DVR capabilities with cloud storage options.

Mobile Technology

Our mobile applications allow customers to stream live TV, access on-demand content, and manage their DVR from anywhere.

We've invested heavily in developing cross-platform solutions that work seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and web browsers.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's history

2005

CableVision was founded with a mission to provide affordable cable TV services to local communities.

2008

Expanded service area to three additional states and introduced high-definition channels to our lineup.

2011

Launched our first digital video recorder (DVR) service, allowing customers to record and pause live TV.

2014

Introduced fiber-optic technology to our network, significantly improving speed and reliability.

2017

Launched our mobile app, allowing customers to stream content on their devices anywhere, anytime.

2020

Introduced 4K Ultra HD channels and expanded our on-demand library to include thousands of titles.

2023

Celebrating serving over 2 million households nationwide with plans for continued expansion and innovation.
